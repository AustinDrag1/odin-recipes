Chili Mac 2 (Convenience)

Description

Make chili, make macaroni, and combine.

This is a convenience meal and should no be here. got to Chili Mac for an actual recipe.

The macaroni is usually elbow or mini bowties. Sometimes cartwheel for fun.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. uncooked macaroni
  • 8 oz. can chili
  • 8 oz. canned beans (kidney, black, pinto, or other)
  • 8 oz. can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1/2 tbsp cayenne powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • (Optional) 2 cups shredded colby jack or mexican 4 cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Cook macaroni according to box. Time will vary by shape and type.
  2. Put chili, beans, and tomatoes in a bot on medium low for 7 minutes. Then lower to low.
  3. Strain pasta.
  4. Bing bong.
  5. Add chili to pasta pot.
  6. Suffer.
